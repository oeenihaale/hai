Jon Grauman is the Director of the Estates Division at The Agency. Jon sold over $45 mil in the 1st quarter of 2017, ranking him among the top agents in the company. As a Los Angeles native, Jon is intimately familiar with the city where he has spent his entire life. He boasts 14 years of experience as a Realtor, Developer and (formerly) Mortgage Broker, and as such, he understands every facet of the real estate business. Jon has extensive experience in construction and development, having developed multiple properties in Beverly Hills and the Sunset Strip. Most notably, Jon was the Developer of Modern/Architectural house designed by acclaimed Architect, Hagy Belzberg. The property, located at 1232 Sunset Plaza Dr, sold for $24 mil and still remains the highest sale ever in Sunset Plaza.

Jon extends his knowledge and expertise to his clients by personally walking them through each stage of the process of buying or selling a home. Communication is one of the keys to a successful transaction, and Jon takes a tremendous amount of pride and gratification in performing at the highest level for his clients and always making himself readily available to them. His professionalism, commitment and attentiveness will be sure to exceed expectations. As a 5th generation Californian, Jon’s family has a rich history in Los Angeles and played an instrumental role in helping build the Entertainment Industry, such as Grauman's Chinese Theater. Prior to Real Estate, Jon’s previous career was in the Music Business where he worked as an A&R Director, Artist Manager, Tour Manager and Promoter with such artists as Guns N' Roses, Mark Foster (Foster The People), Mixmaster Mike (Beastie Boys), Ozzy Osbourne and Marilyn Manson. Jon lives in the Hollywood Hills with my wife and partner, Lauren, their son, Easton, and their dog, Brady.
